📌 Volcano and Adventure Escape

Book Now
Perfect for thrill-seekers and nature lovers, this adventure-packed tour takes you to the iconic Mayon Volcano and the adrenaline-pumping rides of Dahilayan Adventure Park.
Duration:
3 Days / 2 Nights
Destinations:
Mayon Volcano, Dahilayan Adventure Park
Tour Type:
Adventure & Nature
Includes:
Transportation, accommodation, adventure park access, guided tours, breakfast daily

📅 Travel Itinerary

3 Days / 2 Nights

  1. Arrival in Legazpi & Mayon Volcano Tour

    • Arrival in Legazpi City
    • Hotel check-in and briefing
    • Morning: Visit Cagsawa Ruins with Mayon Volcano backdrop
    • Afternoon: Optional ATV ride around Mayon’s lava trails
    • Evening: Dinner at a local restaurant with volcano views
    Overnight in Legazpi

  2. Travel to Bukidnon & Dahilayan Adventure Park

    • Morning flight or transfer to Cagayan de Oro/Bukidnon (via air and land)
    • Check-in near Dahilayan Adventure Park
    • Afternoon: Zipline rides, Forest Luge, Dropzone, and rope courses
    • Evening: Campfire dinner or rest at the lodge
    Overnight in Bukidnon

  3. Nature Walk & Departure

    • Morning: Light nature walk and scenic photo stop
    • Brunch at a hilltop café
    • Transfer to airport for return flight or next destination
    Overnight in Banaue

    End of Tour
