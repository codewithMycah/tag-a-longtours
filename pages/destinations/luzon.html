Luzon
Luzon is the largest island in the Philippines, home to the vibrant capital city, Manila, and natural wonders like mountains, beaches, and rice terraces. It offers a mix of modern city life and rich cultural heritage.

Top Destinations

Vigan
Location Ilocos Sur
Highlights
  • A UNESCO World Heritage Site known for its Spanish colonial architecture, cobblestone streets, and preserved ancestral houses.
  • Activities: Horse-drawn carriage tours, visiting the Crisologo Museum, and shopping for souvenirs.
  • Best Time to Visit: November to March for cooler weather and fewer tourists.
Mayon Volcano
Location Albay, Bicol
Highlights
  • Famous for its perfect cone shape, this active volcano offers breathtaking views and thrilling adventures.
  • Activities: ATV rides, hiking, and photography of the magnificent landscape.
  • Best Time to Visit: Dry season (December to April) to explore comfortably.
Intramuros
Location Manila
Highlights
  • The historic walled city of Manila, showcasing Spanish colonial-era structures and cobblestone pathways.
  • Activities: Walking tours, visiting Fort Santiago and San Agustin Church, and exploring museums.
  • Best Time to Visit: Dry season (December to April) to explore comfortably.
Banaue Rice Terraces
Location Ifugao
Highlights
  • Dubbed the "Eighth Wonder of the World," carved by the Ifugao people over 2,000 years ago.
  • Activities: Trekking, sightseeing, cultural immersion.
  • Best Time to Visit: April–May or October–November.
